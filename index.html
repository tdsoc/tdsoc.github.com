Who are we?

We are a newly-born group of mathematicians, hackers, and cryptographers who are devolving their time and minds to the open source and to the dark side of cryptography, with the aim of developing new tools for applying state-of-the-art cryptanalysis algorithms to real life situations, in order to sensitize the community always to use new-generation and not-broken cryptographic tools.

CryptoLabTN

The dark side of cryptography was born in January 2013, as an dissemination activity of the laboratory of cryptography and industrial mathematics, Department of Mathematics, University of Trento. CryptoLabTN cohordinates the academic effort of the TDSoC, together with other universities, mainly proposing cryptoanalytic attacks to implement and verify. A group of of yung enthusiasts participate freely in the TDSoC, taking parts in the projects they like.

Projects of the TDSoC

The current projects are:

  • Attacks on weak RSA keys
  • Attack on reduced versions of AES up to 6 rounds
The establishment of the new projects follows the guide lines of the TDSoC policies. Ideally, the projects come from publish papers or academic theses.
